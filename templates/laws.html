⚖️ Women Safety Laws in India

Domestic Violence Act (2005)
Protects women from physical, emotional, sexual, and economic abuse by husband or family members. Ensures protection orders and legal relief.
Workplace Harassment Act (2013)
Safeguards women against sexual harassment at the workplace and mandates Internal Complaints Committees (ICC).
Dowry Prohibition Act
Makes giving or taking dowry a punishable offense and protects women from dowry-related cruelty and harassment.
Section 354 IPC
Protects women from assault or criminal force intended to outrage their modesty. Punishable with imprisonment.
IT Act – Cyber Crime
Protects women from online harassment, stalking, morphing, and misuse of personal data on digital platforms.
Protection of Children Act (POCSO)
Protects girls below 18 years from sexual assault, harassment, and exploitation with strict punishment.
